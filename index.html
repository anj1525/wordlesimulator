Wordle



Welcome to Wordle! The rules are simple: input words to try and figure out what the 'right' word is.
If the letter is not in the word, the square will appear with a greyish-pink color. If it is in the word
but in the wrong spot, it will appear in a coral color. Finally, if the letter is in the word and in the
right spot, it will appear in the darker pink color.
Good luck!
